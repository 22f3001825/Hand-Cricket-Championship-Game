How to Play Hand Cricket

Game Flow:

  • The game starts with a toss, followed by two innings.
  • Each innings consists of 1 over (6 balls) or ends when all wickets are down.
  • The team with the highest score after both innings wins!

Toss:

  • Choose Odd or Even
  • Both you and computer select a number (1-6)
  • If the sum matches your choice (Odd or Even), you win the toss
  • Toss winner gets to choose whether to bat or bowl first

Batting:

  • Select a number from 1 to 6 for each ball
  • If your number is different from the computer's, you score runs equal to your number
  • If both numbers are the same, you're out (wicket)!

Bowling:

  • When computer is batting, you bowl by selecting a number (1-6)
  • If computer's number matches yours, it's out!
  • If different, computer scores runs equal to its number
